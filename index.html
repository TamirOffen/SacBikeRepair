Frequently Asked Questions

Where Are You Guys Located?
Since we value our privacy, we will let you know where we can meet up after you have requested a bike service.
Can You Guys Pick-Up My Bike/Drop It Off After You Finish?
It depends upon where you live. When asking for a bike request, add in the notes if you would like a pick-up/drop-off, and we will get back to you.
I Have Not Recieved An Email/Text/Call Back, Why?
There is most likely a variety of things that could have gone wrong: we thought you were spam, we didn't get your service request, etc. Try sending another request and we will try to get back to you.
Can I Have a Custom Service Done To My Bike?
Yes, but it depends upon what that service is. Send a 'Custom Bike Service' request and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
